Fairfield Prep

Fairfield Prep is a high school in Fairfield CT, USA.
It is a Jesuit school based on the principles of St Ignatius of Loyola
Although the school is founded on religion, academics and sports
are also very important, and Fairfield Prep is commited to creating Men for Others
who will be prepared for life beyond.

Music Department

Several Fairfield Prep students take music at least once in their 4 years
The department offers several classes like Orchestra, Band, Choir, and General Music.
